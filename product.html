ERISA Bonds

These bonds are required by the Department of Labor to protect an employee benefit plan against losses caused by acts of fraud or dishonesty.
NEW! Cyber Liability Insurance
We now include Cyber coverage with all 2 and 3 year ERISA bond packages

Colonial is the easy choice.

Colonial Surety is a leading provider of ERISA bonds. We’re licensed in all 50 states and US territories and make it easy to obtain your I-Bond instantly. We also allow you to purchase retroactive insurance for past years where the plan was not covered.

Protect yourself with Fiduciary Liability Insurance (FLI).

Although an ERISA Bond protects the participants of your plan, this does not cover YOU as the fiduciary. So we strongly recommend that you also invest in Fiduciary Liability Insurance, which can be packaged with your ERISA bond. FLI provides you with greater peace of mind, as well as a discount on ERISA coverage.

ERISA bond packages offer the best value.

Packages offer up to $1MM of FLI coverage and provide you with the greatest protection and overall cost savings. Our 2 or 3-year packages also include Cyber Liability Insurance to safeguard your plan assets against a loss due to cyber attack—plus extended coverage to ensure your bond remains Department of Labor compliant.
